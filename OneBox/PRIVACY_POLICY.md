# OneBox Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Our Privacy Commitment

OneBox is built with privacy as our fundamental principle. We believe your files and data should stay private and under your complete control.

## Data Collection: None

**We collect NO data whatsoever from OneBox users.**

- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No file contents or metadata
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No crash reports (unless you explicitly send them)
- ❌ No device identifiers
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No contact information
- ❌ No browsing history
- ❌ No advertising identifiers

## How OneBox Protects Your Privacy

### 100% On-Device Processing
- All file processing happens entirely on your iPhone or iPad
- Your files never leave your device during processing
- No cloud uploads, ever
- No external servers involved in core functionality

### No Network Activity for Core Features
- PDF creation, merging, compression: 100% offline
- Image processing and conversion: 100% offline
- File organization and management: 100% offline
- Document signing: 100% offline

### Apple-Only Technologies
OneBox uses exclusively Apple frameworks and technologies:
- **PDFKit** for PDF manipulation
- **Core Graphics** for image processing
- **Core Spotlight** for on-device search
- **StoreKit** for in-app purchases
- **Vision Framework** for document analysis (on-device AI)

### Secure File Handling
- Files are processed in secure temporary directories
- Temporary files are automatically deleted after processing
- No file caching or permanent storage outside your control
- Uses iOS security-scoped resources for file access

## What Data Stays on Your Device

The following information remains stored locally on your device only:

### App Preferences
- Tool settings and configurations
- Export limits and usage tracking (for free tier)
- Automation workflows you create
- Your signature drawings (stored in local keychain)

### File Processing History
- List of recently processed files (file names only)
- Processing job queue and status
- Export history for your reference

All of this data:
- ✅ Stays on your device
- ✅ Is not transmitted anywhere
- ✅ Can be deleted by removing the app
- ✅ Is not accessible to other apps

## Third-Party Services: None

OneBox does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud providers for its core functionality.

### In-App Purchases
- Handled directly by Apple's StoreKit
- We never see your payment information
- Apple manages all transaction data
- Purchase validation happens through Apple's servers only

## Permissions We Request

### Photo Library Access
- **Purpose**: To convert your photos to PDF or process images
- **Scope**: Only files you explicitly select
- **Usage**: Files are processed immediately and temporarily
- **Storage**: No permanent copies are made

### File System Access
- **Purpose**: To access PDFs and documents you want to process
- **Scope**: Only files you explicitly select through iOS file picker
- **Usage**: Files are read for processing only
- **Storage**: No copies are made outside temporary processing

## Children's Privacy

OneBox does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

## Data Retention

Since we collect no data, there is nothing to retain. All processing happens locally and temporarily on your device.

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device:

- **Access**: All your data is on your device in the OneBox app
- **Deletion**: Delete the app to remove all OneBox data
- **Portability**: Export your processed files to any app or service you choose
- **Transparency**: This privacy policy covers everything - there are no hidden data practices

## Changes to This Policy

3. Never reduce privacy protections without explicit user consent
